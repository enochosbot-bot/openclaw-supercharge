## How to Add

When you notice something that could be better:
1. Add an entry in the appropriate priority section
2. Include enough context that future-you understands
3. Estimate effort honestly
4. Don't gold-plate â€” if it's not worth doing, don't add it

## Review Schedule

- **Weekly:** Scan for quick wins (low effort, high impact)
- **Monthly:** Prioritize medium items, clear completed/wontfix
- **Quarterly:** Review high priority items that haven't moved
